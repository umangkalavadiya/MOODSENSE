MOODSENSE

A student emotion-based faculty feedback system is a novel approach that utilizes technology to gather valuable insights from students about their courses and instructors. By allowing students to express their emotions and opinions, educators can better understand their teaching methods and make data-driven improvements. This system empowers students to voice their feelings and helps faculty create a more engaging and supportive learning environment. Ultimately, it fosters a stronger connection between students and educators, enhancing the overall educational experience.
